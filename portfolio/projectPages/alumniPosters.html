Alumni Posters

This poster was designed for RIT's Women in Computing club. Working with a few other members on the Public Relations committee, we designed a series of informational posters about RIT Alumni who were in the club.

It includes information about who they are, what they've been up to since graduating, and where they are currently worknig. The reason behind the poster design was to promote the club as well as show women in technology different paths previous graduates have taken. You can find more here.
