Tennis Poster

I designed this poster for RIT's Tennis Club. As vice president, my e-board and I recognized there needed to be more awareness on campus about our club in order to attract members. The poster needed to be eye catching and informational. The design was kept simple in order to not overwhelm the viewer, and information was provided in order to contact us with any questions, which many people did.
